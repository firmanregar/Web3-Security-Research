# Publications

This section contains long-form publications and educational materials related to smart contract security and the broader Web3 ecosystem.

## Web3 Security Essentials: Your Journey and Roadmap to Mastering the Art and Science of Smart Contract Security

This publication provides a structured introduction to the field of smart contract security, outlining the key concepts, learning paths, and practical strategies required to become an effective security researcher in the Web3 ecosystem.

Topics covered include:

* fundamentals of blockchain security
* common smart contract vulnerabilities
* DeFi attack surfaces
* auditing methodologies
* practical learning roadmap for aspiring auditors

The goal of this publication is to help newcomers and researchers better understand the evolving landscape of smart contract security.
